Welcome to Secretbox.

SecretBox is a fundamental change to the way people store files in the Cloud.

When you use it, we make a strong promise:
Nobody can ever read, edit, or delete your files.

We guarantee this by combining the storage of your existing cloud services with cryptographic and redundancy algorithms.

These services, such as Dropbox and Google Drive, are what we call "providers". You'll add some in just a minute.
